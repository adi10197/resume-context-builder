# Product Manager Resume Type

This resume type is optimized for Product Management positions with focus on strategy, execution, and business impact.

## Specific Rules

### Target Audience
- Product Manager positions (Associate to Senior/Director level)
- Technical Product Manager roles
- Group Product Manager positions
- Product Owner roles
- Product Lead positions

### Emphasis Areas
1. **Business Impact** - Revenue, growth, efficiency, user metrics
2. **Product Strategy** - Vision, roadmap, prioritization, market analysis
3. **Execution** - Shipping products, cross-functional leadership, delivery
4. **User Focus** - Research, customer empathy, problem discovery
5. **Data-Driven Decisions** - Analytics, experimentation, metrics definition
6. **Stakeholder Management** - Communication, alignment, influence without authority

### Section Order
1. Header (Name and Contact Info)
2. Professional Summary (recommended - highlights product philosophy)
3. Professional Experience
4. Skills
5. Education
6. Certifications (optional - product management courses, etc.)

## Professional Summary Format

Include a 2-3 sentence summary highlighting PM approach:
```markdown
## Professional Summary

Product leader with 7+ years of experience building B2B SaaS products from 0-to-1 and scaling existing products to $50M+ ARR. Combines deep customer empathy with data-driven decision making to deliver products that solve real problems. Track record of leading cross-functional teams through ambiguity to successful launches.
```

## Skills Section Format

Focus on PM-relevant competencies:
```markdown
## Skills

**Product**: Product strategy, roadmap planning, prioritization frameworks, PRD writing, user story mapping, competitive analysis
**Research**: User interviews, usability testing, survey design, jobs-to-be-done, persona development
**Analytics**: SQL, Amplitude, Mixpanel, A/B testing, cohort analysis, funnel optimization
**Execution**: Agile/Scrum, sprint planning, stakeholder management, go-to-market coordination
**Technical**: API design (conceptual), system design (conceptual), technical trade-off discussions
**Tools**: Jira, Linear, Figma, Notion, Productboard, Amplitude, Looker
```

## Experience Bullet Point Guidelines

### Prioritize
- Business impact with metrics (revenue, growth, retention, efficiency)
- Product launches and outcomes
- Strategic decisions and their impact
- Cross-functional leadership
- User research insights that drove decisions
- Data-driven experimentation
- Stakeholder alignment achievements
- Problem discovery and definition

### De-emphasize
- Technical implementation details
- Day-to-day project management tasks
- Process documentation
- Meetings and coordination (show outcomes instead)

### Example Bullet Points (Business Impact)
- Led product strategy for payments platform, growing transaction volume from $10M to $100M annually and increasing revenue by 150%
- Launched self-serve onboarding flow that reduced time-to-value from 14 days to 2 hours, improving trial-to-paid conversion by 35%
- Defined and executed retention roadmap that reduced churn from 8% to 4% monthly, saving $5M ARR
- Identified and prioritized enterprise features through customer research, enabling expansion into new market segment worth $20M TAM

### Example Bullet Points (Execution & Leadership)
- Shipped 12 major features in 18 months with cross-functional team of 8 engineers, 2 designers, and 3 stakeholder groups
- Drove alignment between Sales, Marketing, and Engineering on product roadmap, resolving competing priorities through structured prioritization framework
- Established product discovery practices including weekly user interviews and monthly usability testing, improving feature success rate from 40% to 75%
- Led 0-to-1 product development from concept through beta launch with 50 design partners to GA with 500+ paying customers

### Example Bullet Points (Data & Research)
- Designed and analyzed 30+ A/B experiments, driving data-informed decisions that increased key metrics by 20%+
- Conducted 100+ customer interviews to identify Jobs-to-be-Done, uncovering $10M opportunity that became top roadmap priority
- Built product analytics framework defining North Star metric and supporting KPIs, adopted by 3 product teams

## Technologies Section per Role

For each position, include product and domain context:
```markdown
**Product Area**: Payments platform, B2B SaaS, API product
**Methods**: User research, A/B testing, SQL analytics, Agile/Scrum
```

## Job Description Tailoring

When job description is provided:
- Match product type (B2B vs B2C, platform vs application)
- Emphasize relevant domain experience (fintech, healthcare, etc.)
- Highlight 0-to-1 vs growth experience as relevant
- Feature technical depth if Technical PM role
- Match company stage (startup vs enterprise)
- Emphasize data skills if analytics-heavy role

## What to Minimize
- Technical implementation details
- Engineering management activities
- Administrative tasks
- Tools and technologies (focus on outcomes)

## What to Highlight by PM Type

### Technical PM
- API and platform product experience
- Technical trade-off discussions
- Developer experience and documentation
- Integration partnerships

### Growth PM
- Experimentation and A/B testing
- Funnel optimization
- Acquisition and activation metrics
- Data analysis depth

### 0-to-1 PM
- Product discovery and validation
- MVP definition and iteration
- Market analysis and positioning
- Ambiguity navigation

### Platform PM
- Internal tools and developer experience
- API design and documentation
- Multi-team coordination
- Technical debt prioritization
