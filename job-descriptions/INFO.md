## Instructions for Using This File

When you paste a real job description:
1. Keep the original formatting and content intact
2. Include all sections (responsibilities, qualifications, tech stack, etc.)
3. Note any specific keywords or phrases used repeatedly
4. Pay attention to required vs preferred qualifications
5. Look for clues about company culture and values

The AI will use this to:
- Match your experience to job requirements
- Include relevant keywords for ATS optimization
- Highlight applicable skills and technologies
- Tailor bullet points to job responsibilities
- Use similar language and terminology
